Facebook Privacy Tips

Enhance your privacy on Facebook with these tips:

  • Adjust Privacy Settings

    Regularly review and customize your Facebook privacy settings. Control who can see your posts, friend requests, and personal information.

  • Manage Friend Requests

    Be selective about friend requests. Only accept requests from people you know and trust to maintain a secure friend network.

  • Secure Your Profile Information

    Limit the visibility of your profile information, such as your phone number and email address. Only share what you are comfortable with.

  • Use Strong Passwords

    Protect your account with a strong and unique password. Avoid using easily guessable information like your birthdate or common words.

  • Review App Permissions

    Check and manage the permissions granted to third-party apps connected to your Facebook account. Remove unnecessary apps to enhance security.
